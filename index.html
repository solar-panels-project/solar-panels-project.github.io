Introduction

We were tasked with figuring out a way to make APL more environmentally friendly. After a few ideas were debated within our group, we settled on coming up with the concept of solar panels at APL.

Benefits of Solar Power

  • Savings

    Electricity is expensive. Having solar panels in the parking lot of APL would help cut the cost of electricty at the lab.

  • Comfort

    Solar panels provide shade. This could help keep the cars cool during the summer. Cars are also protected in the rain.

  • Sustainability

    Implementing solar panels also will help contribute to APL becoming a greener campus. Electricity generation through fossil fuels contributes green house emissions.

Solar Power Cost

Cost about $1 per watt for non tracking solar panels
The average cost of a 6 kilowatt solar system in Maryland is $15,540 according to Consumer Affairs
Tracking systems are about $5000, so the total installation cost is $20,540 according to Diy Solar Shack
According to Global Solar Atlas there are 1549 peak sun hours in Laurel, Maryland. 1549 * 6kW = 9294 kWh. Two axis trackers are about 46% more efficient Source
9294 + (9294 * .46) = 13,569.24 kWh on a two axis tracker
Using a rate of 7.83 cents per kWh, $1,062.47 can be saved per year per panel
Installation cost per panel would be payed off in 19.33 years.
